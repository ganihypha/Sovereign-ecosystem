## 3. CONVERSION PATH (Jalur Konversi Detail)

### Path A: Cold Lead → Reseller (Main Path)
```
1. Scout Agent temukan IG account (ScraperAPI)
2. AI scoring: HOT (score >= 80)
3. Closer Agent: Day 0 WA via Fonnte
4. Lead reply → Manual follow-up / AI compose
5. Demo produk FashionKas
6. Day 3 follow-up jika tidak reply
7. Reseller signup → Order pertama
8. Day 7/14 retention sequence
```
**Estimasi conversion rate: 15-25%**
**Estimasi waktu: 3-14 hari per lead**

## 5. KPI DASHBOARD (Key Performance Indicators)

### 5.1 Revenue KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Target Minggu Ini | Target Akhir Bulan |
|-----|---------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Weekly Revenue | SUM(orders.total_amount WHERE status=completed AND week=current) | Rp 3 jt | Rp 10 jt |
| Monthly Recurring Revenue | SUM(orders per bulan) | - | Rp 40 jt |
| Average Order Value | SUM(revenue) / COUNT(orders) | Rp 500K | Rp 750K |
| Revenue Growth % | (this_week - last_week) / last_week × 100 | +15% | +20% |
| Unpaid Invoices | SUM(orders WHERE status IN [pending, processing]) | < Rp 2 jt | < 10% of monthly |

### 5.2 Lead & Conversion KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Target Minggu Ini | Target Akhir Bulan |
|-----|---------|-------------------|-------------------|
| New Leads Discovered | COUNT(leads WHERE created_at=this_week) | 30 leads | 120 leads |
| HOT Leads Rate | COUNT(leads WHERE score>=80) / COUNT(leads) × 100 | >40% | >50% |
| Outreach Rate | COUNT(leads WHERE status=contacted) / COUNT(leads WHERE score>=70) | >60% | >80% |
| Lead-to-Reseller CVR | COUNT(leads WHERE status=converted) / COUNT(leads) × 100 | 10% | 25% |
| WA Response Rate | COUNT(wa_logs WHERE status=replied) / COUNT(wa_logs WHERE status=sent) | >20% | >30% |

## 8. WEEKLY REVIEW RITUAL (Protokol Review Mingguan)

Setiap Senin pagi, Founder melakukan review 30 menit:

### Checklist Review Mingguan
```
📊 REVENUE CHECK (5 menit)
  □ Revenue minggu lalu vs target?
  □ Revenue growth % vs minggu sebelumnya?
  □ Unpaid invoices perlu di-follow up?
  □ Product apa yang paling banyak terjual?

🎯 LEAD PIPELINE CHECK (10 menit)
  □ Berapa leads baru ditemukan minggu lalu?
  □ Berapa yang di-score HOT?
  □ Berapa yang sudah di-outreach?
  □ Berapa yang respond? Berapa yang convert?
  □ Lead HOT yang belum di-contact → outreach sekarang!

🤖 AI PERFORMANCE CHECK (5 menit)
  □ AI insights yang dihasilkan — berapa yang actionable?
  □ Scout Agent scoring accuracy — ada yang salah score?
  □ MessageComposer — pesan mana yang paling tinggi response rate?
  □ CrewAI weekly report — insight terpenting apa?

📱 3-LAYER VALIDATION CHECK (5 menit)
  □ @fashionkas: Berapa reseller baru signup minggu ini?
  □ @resellerkas: Engagement rate naik atau turun?

🎯 NEXT WEEK PRIORITIES (5 menit)
```
