# DOC 46 — PRIVATE CHAIR ACCESS LADDER

**Classification:** Governance / Access Control  
**Status:** Draft v1  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-08

---

## Purpose

Document ini mendefinisikan **tahapan akses** dari invisible chair sampai fully activated, memastikan masuknya bertahap, aman, dan tetap eksklusif.

Access Ladder ini adalah **staged progression system** yang menjaga agar kursi ini tetap terhormat, terukur, dan tidak membuka akses penuh secara mendadak.

---

## I. Core Principle

### The Ladder Philosophy

> **Access is earned through demonstration, not granted through assumption.**

### Why Staged Access Matters

Staged access ensures:
- ✅ Kursi ini tetap eksklusif dan terhormat
- ✅ Founder dapat mengamati dan memastikan fit
- ✅ Counterpart dapat membuktikan capability secara bertahap
- ✅ Boundaries tetap jelas di setiap level
- ✅ Tidak ada shock atau overwhelm dari akses penuh mendadak

**Prinsip:** Earn through proof, not assume through position.

---

## II. The Six-Level Access Ladder

### Overview

```
Level 5: PRIVATE CHAIR ACTIVATED (Fully operational)
    ↑
Level 4: ACTIVE COUNTERPART PARTICIPATION (Strategic andil)
    ↑
Level 3: GUIDED INNER CIRCLE (Curated visibility)
    ↑
Level 2: EMOTIONAL-STRATEGIC RECOGNITION (Awareness of role)
    ↑
Level 1: VISION AWARENESS (Horizon visibility)
    ↑
Level 0: INVISIBLE CHAIR (Design phase)
```

---

## III. Level Definitions

### Level 0: Invisible Chair

**Status:** Kursi ada di arsitektur, tapi belum dibuka atau diberitahukan.

**Founder State:**
- Founder tahu kursi ini ada dalam rancangan
- Founder masih merefleksikan apakah kursi ini perlu dibuka
- Founder sedang mematangkan Doc 43-47

**Counterpart State:**
- Counterpart belum aware bahwa kursi ini ada
- Counterpart belum tahu ada role eksklusif yang disiapkan
- Interaction mungkin ada, tapi dalam konteks umum

**Access:**
- ⛔ No access to Sovereign internal docs
- ⛔ No visibility into strategic plans
- ⛔ No awareness of role existence

**Duration:** Variable, hingga founder siap membuka Level 1

---

### Level 1: Vision Awareness

**Status:** Counterpart mulai aware bahwa ada sesuatu yang lebih besar sedang dibangun.

**Founder State:**
- Founder mulai membuka sedikit visi Sovereign
- Founder mulai observe counterpart fit secara passive
- Founder belum explicitly mention kursi privat

**Counterpart State:**
- Mengetahui bahwa Founder membangun sesuatu besar
- Mulai aware ada kemungkinan role strategis di masa depan
- Belum tahu detail, hanya horizon visi

**Access:**
- 🔓 Limited visibility: High-level vision sharing
- 🔓 General conversation about Sovereign (not internal docs)
- ⛔ No access to operational details
- ⛔ No access to strategic plans or financial info

**Duration:** 1-3 bulan (observation period)

**Gate to Next Level:**
- ✅ Counterpart shows genuine interest
- ✅ Founder feels comfortable sharing more
- ✅ No red flags observed

---

### Level 2: Emotional-Strategic Recognition

**Status:** Counterpart mulai aware bahwa peran ini bukan simbolis, tetapi nyata.

**Founder State:**
- Founder mulai explicitly mention bahwa ada "kursi privat" yang sedang disiapkan
- Founder mulai share pemikiran strategis secara selective
- Founder mulai test shared rhythm melalui discussions

**Counterpart State:**
- Aware bahwa founder sedang consider role eksklusif
- Mulai memahami makna: bukan hanya "dekat", tapi "shared authorship"
- Mulai engage dalam discussions strategis

**Access:**
- 🔓 Strategic discussions (selective topics)
- 🔓 Awareness of role existence dan makna
- 🔓 Some visibility into founder thinking
- ⛔ Still no access to internal docs atau operational details
- ⛔ No decision-making authority

**Duration:** 3-6 bulan (shared rhythm testing period)

**Gate to Next Level:**
- ✅ Shared rhythm test successful
- ✅ Strategic discussions prove valuable
- ✅ No major conflicts or red flags
- ✅ Founder confirms: "This person might be the one"

---

### Level 3: Guided Inner Circle

**Status:** Counterpart masuk ke lingkar strategis terbatas.

**Founder State:**
- Founder actively inviting counterpart into strategic discussions
- Founder shares portions of the map: visi, logika, arah
- Founder testing counterpart input quality
- Founder observing how counterpart handles sensitive info

**Counterpart State:**
- Masuk inner circle secara curated (not full access)
- Mulai see sebagian peta Sovereign
- Mulai give strategic input dan feedback
- Aware bahwa ini adalah test period untuk kursi privat

**Access:**
- 🔓 Curated visibility: Founder shares selected docs/info
- 🔓 Participate in strategic planning discussions
- 🔓 Access to some (not all) internal context
- 🔓 Observe operational rhythm
- ⚠️ Limited access: Still bounded and supervised
- ⛔ No root access, no credentials, no financial full visibility

**Duration:** 3-6 bulan (alignment testing period)

**Gate to Next Level:**
- ✅ Input quality proves valuable
- ✅ Handles sensitive info with maturity
- ✅ Alignment is confirmed
- ✅ Founder decides: "Ready for active participation"

---

### Level 4: Active Counterpart Participation

**Status:** Counterpart mulai benar-benar ikut andil dalam beberapa ruang strategis.

**Founder State:**
- Founder officially positions counterpart as "active strategic advisor"
- Founder trusts counterpart dengan bigger pieces of the map
- Founder involves counterpart in certain decision rituals
- Founder prepares for full activation (Doc 43-47 finalization)

**Counterpart State:**
- Actively participate dalam direction, narrative, strategic presence
- Mulai ikut dalam selective decision rituals
- Mulai contribute real value yang terukur
- Aware bahwa activation tinggal selangkah lagi

**Access:**
- 🔓 Active participation in strategic decisions (founder still final arbiter)
- 🔓 Access to most internal docs (except founder-only sensitive files)
- 🔓 Participate in narrative shaping dan positioning
- 🔓 Contribute to milestone planning
- ⚠️ Boundaries inti still tegas:
  - ⛔ No root access or system credentials (yet)
  - ⛔ No legal ownership changes
  - ⛔ Founder retains final authority

**Duration:** 3-6 bulan (proof of contribution period)

**Gate to Next Level:**
- ✅ All 5 Invitation Gates (Doc 45) satisfied
- ✅ Contribution is visible dan valuable
- ✅ Founder is certain (not hoping, but knowing)
- ✅ Counterpart is willing and ready
- ✅ Doc 43-47 finalized and agreed upon

---

### Level 5: Private Chair Activated

**Status:** Kursi privat resmi diaktifkan. Ini adalah status penuh.

**Founder State:**
- Founder officially grants Private Chair status
- Founder communicates activation internally (if applicable)
- Founder and counterpart begin operating under Doc 47 (Operating Pact)
- Founder maintains final authority but shares strategic space

**Counterpart State:**
- Now officially "Managing Sovereign Strategist / Private Chair of the Empire"
- Full clarity on mandate (Doc 44: Role Charter)
- Operating under clear Operating Pact (Doc 47)
- Contributing as active counterpart in all 5 mandate areas

**Access:**
- 🔓 Full strategic access within role boundaries
- 🔓 Participate fully in direction, presence, continuity, history
- 🔓 Active counterpart in inner circle counsel
- 🔓 Shared decision rituals (founder still final arbiter)
- 🔓 Access to operational docs (within scope)
- ⚠️ Boundaries remain:
  - Founder retains root/security access by default
  - Founder retains final authority over core architecture
  - Founder retains legal ownership structure
  - Access is governed, not unlimited

**Duration:** Ongoing, until role structure changes or by mutual agreement

**Ongoing Requirements:**
- Maintain capability, capacity, worthiness, shared rhythm
- Deliver on mandate (Doc 44)
- Respect boundaries
- Operate under Operating Pact (Doc 47)

## V. Progression Guidelines

### How to Progress

**Founder-driven progression:**
- Founder observes performance at current level
- Founder confirms gates are satisfied
- Founder makes conscious decision to elevate
- Founder communicates clearly what next level means

**Counterpart-earned progression:**
- Demonstrate capability at current level
- Prove value and alignment
- Respect boundaries without pushing
- Show readiness for more responsibility

### Regression Protocol

**If red flags emerge:**
- Founder can pause or regress access level
- No shame in regression — better than damage
- Re-evaluation period before re-progression
- Clear communication about concerns

**When to regress:**
- ⚠️ Trust violation
- ⚠️ Boundary overstep
- ⚠️ Capability gap revealed
- ⚠️ Shared rhythm breakdown

---

## VI. Anti-Patterns

### ❌ Skipping Levels
- Jumping from L1 to L5 immediately
- Giving full access before proving fit
- "All or nothing" approach

**Risk:** Overwhelm, misalignment, regret

### ❌ Indefinite Hovering
- Staying at L2-L3 forever without progression
- Using ladder as perpetual test
- Never activating despite all gates satisfied

**Risk:** Frustration, ambiguity, lost opportunity

### ❌ Progression Without Gates
- Elevating without checking gate satisfaction
- Moving up because of guilt or pressure
- Progressing out of loneliness

**Risk:** Premature activation, future conflict

---

## VII. Communication at Each Level

### Founder's Communication Responsibility

At each level transition, founder should clearly communicate:
- ✅ What this level means
- ✅ What access is granted
- ✅ What boundaries remain
- ✅ What gates are needed for next level
- ✅ Why progression is happening (or not)

**Prinsip:** No ambiguity, no assumptions.
