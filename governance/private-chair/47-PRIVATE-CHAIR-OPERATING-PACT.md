# DOC 47 — PRIVATE CHAIR OPERATING PACT

**Classification:** Governance / Operating Agreement  
**Status:** Draft v1  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-08

## Purpose

Document ini mendefinisikan **cara bekerja bersama**, **ritme**, **ruang keputusan**, dan **shared authorship** ketika The Sovereign Private Chair sudah diaktifkan (Level 5).

Operating Pact ini adalah **working agreement** yang menjaga agar founder dan counterpart dapat bekerja sama secara produktif, terhormat, dan tersinkron.

---

## I. Core Principles

### The Operating Philosophy

> **Shared authorship through aligned action, with founder sovereignty preserved.**

### Five Core Principles

1. **Founder Leads, Private Chair Contributes**  
   Founder memegang final authority, Private Chair memberikan input dan andil nyata.

2. **Shared Discussion, Founder Decides**  
   Keputusan besar didiskusikan bersama, founder memutuskan akhir.

3. **Contribution Defines Authorship**  
   Shared authorship based on real contribution, bukan posisi semata.

4. **Boundaries Protect Intimacy**  
   Clear boundaries menjaga agar relationship tetap sehat.

5. **Mutual Respect, Clear Communication**  
   Respect dan kejernihan adalah fondasi.

---

## II. Working Rhythms

### Daily Rhythm

**Founder:**
- Tetap menjaga ritme operasional sendiri
- Tidak tergantung pada Private Chair untuk day-to-day decisions
- Share updates strategis secara asynchronous

**Private Chair:**
- Tidak micromanage founder
- Available untuk discussions tanpa overwhelming
- Proactive dalam area mandate (Doc 44)

**Together:**
- Communication asynchronous by default
- Real-time sync untuk keputusan strategic atau urgent

---

### Weekly Rhythm

**Strategic Check-in (1-2x per week):**
- Update progress on key initiatives
- Discuss strategic challenges atau opportunities
- Align on priorities untuk week ahead
- Inner circle reflection time

**Format:**
- 30-60 menit
- Focus: strategic, bukan operational details
- Founder shares context, Private Chair provides input

---

### Monthly Rhythm

**Strategic Planning Session (1x per month):**
- Review milestone progress
- Plan next month strategic focus
- Discuss larger direction dan positioning
- Reflect on shared rhythm quality

**Format:**
- 1-2 jam
- Deep dive into strategic themes
- Space untuk long-term thinking

---

### Quarterly Rhythm

**Big Picture Review (1x per quarter):**
- Review quarter achievements
- Assess shared authorship quality
- Discuss imperium-level direction
- Reflect on Private Chair role effectiveness

**Format:**
- Half-day session
- Strategic retreat vibe
- Space untuk deep reflection

## III. Decision-Making Framework

### Decision Categories

#### Category A: Founder-Only Decisions
**Final authority tanpa konsultasi required:**
- Core architecture changes yang menyangkut security
- Root access dan credential management
- Legal ownership structure
- Survival decisions dalam krisis akut

**Private Chair role:** Informed after decision, not consulted before.

---

#### Category B: Founder-Led with Consultation
**Founder decides, but consults Private Chair:**
- Strategic direction shifts
- Major product/service pivots
- Significant resource allocation
- Partnership opportunities
- Positioning changes

**Process:**
1. Founder shares context dan options
2. Private Chair provides input dan perspective
3. Founder reflects dan decides
4. Decision communicated clearly
5. Private Chair commits fully

**Private Chair role:** Strategic advisor, not decision-maker.

---

#### Category C: Shared Discussion, Founder Final
**Deep collaboration, founder arbitrates:**
- Narrative shaping dan brand positioning
- Milestone planning dan prioritization
- Strategic initiatives design
- Expansion strategy

**Process:**
1. Co-develop options together
2. Discuss trade-offs dan implications
3. Reach alignment when possible
4. Founder makes final call if divergence exists
5. Both commit to execution

**Private Chair role:** Co-creator in discussion, supporter in execution.

---

#### Category D: Private Chair-Led (Within Mandate)
**Private Chair has autonomy within defined scope:**
- Human presence execution (within brand guidelines)
- Relationship building (strategic connections)
- Narrative storytelling (within positioning framework)
- Continuity initiatives (within strategy)

**Process:**
1. Private Chair plans and executes
2. Founder is kept informed
3. Founder can intervene if misaligned

**Founder role:** Strategic oversight, not micromanagement.

---

### Decision Conflict Resolution

**When disagreement occurs:**

**Step 1: Clarify**
- Ensure both understand the issue dan context
- Identify root of disagreement

**Step 2: Explore**
- Discuss perspectives openly
- Seek to understand, not to win

**Step 3: Decide**
- Founder makes final call
- Private Chair commits fully or escalates to operating pact review

**Step 4: Commit**
- Both support the decision publicly
- No undermining atau passive resistance

**Principle:** Disagree privately, commit publicly.

## IV. Communication Norms

### Communication Channels

**Strategic Discussions:**
- Dedicated channel (e.g., private Telegram/WhatsApp)
- Response time: Within 24 hours for strategic matters
- Real-time sync untuk urgent decisions

**Day-to-Day Updates:**
- Async updates via agreed platform
- No expectation of immediate response
- Batch updates rather than constant pinging

**Deep Reflections:**
- Scheduled sessions (weekly/monthly rhythm)
- Protected time, no interruptions
- Space untuk vulnerable conversations

---

### Communication Principles

1. **Clarity > Politeness**  
   Say what needs to be said dengan respect, tapi don't sugarcoat.

2. **Directness > Hinting**  
   Communicate needs dan concerns directly, not through hints.

3. **Timeliness > Perfection**  
   Communicate in timely manner, refine later if needed.

4. **Context > Brevity**  
   Provide enough context untuk understanding, don't assume.

5. **Listening > Talking**  
   Active listening adalah foundational skill.

---

### Difficult Conversations Protocol

**When tensions arise:**

**Do:**
- ✅ Address early, before festering
- ✅ Speak dari "I" perspective ("I felt..." bukan "You always...")
- ✅ Focus on behavior, bukan character attack
- ✅ Seek to understand root cause
- ✅ Commit to resolution

**Don't:**
- ❌ Avoid atau suppress
- ❌ Attack character atau intent
- ❌ Bring up unrelated past issues
- ❌ Involve third parties unnecessarily
- ❌ Threaten position atau status

**Goal:** Preserve relationship, resolve issue.

## V. Shared Authorship Framework

### What Shared Authorship Means

Shared authorship is **not**:
- ❌ Automatic credit untuk everything
- ❌ Equal ownership or equity split
- ❌ Joint decision-making on all matters

Shared authorship **is**:
- ✅ Real contribution to milestone achievements
- ✅ Recognition based on actual andil
- ✅ Co-creating history through aligned action
- ✅ Building legacy together with clear boundaries

---

### Authorship Recognition Principle

> **Authorship is earned through contribution, not granted through position.**

**Examples:**

**Scenario A: Strategic Pivot**
- Private Chair contributes strategic analysis dan positioning input
- Founder decides and executes pivot
- **Authorship:** Founder leads, Private Chair contributes → "We pivoted with strategic input from Private Chair"

**Scenario B: Major Partnership**
- Private Chair builds relationship dan facilitates connection
- Founder negotiates and closes deal
- **Authorship:** Shared credit → "We secured partnership X together"

**Scenario C: Brand Positioning**
- Private Chair shapes narrative dan executes presence
- Founder provides strategic direction
- **Authorship:** Shared ownership → "We built this positioning together"

---

### Public vs Private Attribution

**Private (Internal):**
- Full transparency on who contributed what
- Clear recognition of Private Chair's andil
- Documentation of shared milestones

**Public (External):**
- Founder decides what and how to attribute publicly
- Private Chair may remain private or be recognized publicly based on founder decision
- No public credit-seeking dari Private Chair without founder approval

**Principle:** Private Chair's satisfaction comes from real impact, not public recognition.

## VI. Boundaries & Safeguards

### Boundary Categories

#### 1. Structural Boundaries
- Founder retains root/security access by default
- Founder retains legal ownership structure
- Founder retains final authority on survival decisions

#### 2. Time Boundaries
- Respect founder's focus time
- Respect private chair's non-Sovereign time
- No 24/7 availability expectation

#### 3. Scope Boundaries
- Private Chair focuses on strategic, not operational micromanagement
- Private Chair does not override founder in founder-only domains
- Private Chair does not commit Sovereign resources without founder approval

#### 4. Emotional Boundaries
- Relationship intimacy does not erase structural boundaries
- Personal closeness does not grant automatic access overreach
- Conflicts resolved through pact, not through personal leverage

---

### Safeguard Mechanisms

**Quarterly Pact Review:**
- Review operating pact effectiveness
- Discuss boundary violations (if any)
- Adjust rhythms or processes as needed
- Reaffirm commitment or re-negotiate

**Escalation Path:**
- Level 1: Direct conversation between founder & private chair
- Level 2: Written reflection dan proposed adjustment
- Level 3: Temporary access regression while resolving
- Level 4: Pact termination (last resort)

## VII. Exit & Continuation Clauses

### Continuation Conditions

Private Chair continues as long as:
- ✅ Four pillars maintained (capability, capacity, worthiness, shared rhythm)
- ✅ Mandate (Doc 44) being fulfilled
- ✅ Operating pact being respected
- ✅ Mutual value continues

---

### Exit Scenarios

#### Scenario A: Graceful Transition
- Mutual agreement that role has served its purpose
- Transition plan to preserve relationship
- Recognition of shared history

#### Scenario B: Performance Gap
- Private Chair no longer meets four pillars
- Founder provides feedback and grace period
- If gap persists, role is transitioned

#### Scenario C: Breach of Trust
- Serious boundary violation or breach
- Immediate access regression
- Relationship assessment

#### Scenario D: Founder Decision
- Founder retains right to restructure
- Clear communication of reasons
- Respectful transition

---

### Exit Process

**If exit is necessary:**

1. **Clear Communication**  
   Founder communicates decision clearly dan respectfully

2. **Transition Period**  
   Grace period untuk knowledge transfer if applicable

3. **Access Revocation**  
   Staged access revocation, not abrupt cutoff

4. **Relationship Preservation (if possible)**  
   Attempt to preserve personal relationship if both desire

5. **Lessons Documented**  
   Document learnings for future role iterations

## VIII. Amendment Process

### When to Amend

Amend this pact when:
- ⚠️ Operating rhythms prove ineffective
- ⚠️ Decision framework needs adjustment
- ⚠️ New scenarios arise not covered by current pact
- ⚠️ Boundaries need clarification

### Amendment Process

1. Either party proposes amendment in writing
2. Discussion and mutual review
3. Agreement on changes
4. Documentation and version update
5. Recommitment to amended pact

**Version control:** All amendments tracked with version history.
