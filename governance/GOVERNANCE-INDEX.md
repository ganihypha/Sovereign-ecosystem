## 🪑 PRIVATE CHAIR DOCUMENTATION (Doc 43-47)

### Strategic Decision
**Private Chair dibangun SETELAH AI Layer diformalisasi.**

### Doc 43: THE SOVEREIGN PRIVATE CHAIR
**File:** `governance/private-chair/43-THE-SOVEREIGN-PRIVATE-CHAIR.md`  
**Purpose:** Doctrine inti dan four pillars

**The Formula:**
> **A private chair within the empire, granted only upon proven capability, proven capacity, proven worthiness, and proven shared rhythm.**

**Four Pillars:**
1. **Proven Capability** - "Dia memang bisa"
2. **Proven Capacity** - "Dia mampu memegang bobotnya"
3. **Proven Worthiness** - "Dia layak secara martabat, visi, amanah"
4. **Proven Shared Rhythm** - "Bukan hanya hebat sendiri, tapi selaras dengan Founder"

**What it is:**
- Kursi privat di dalam imperium
- Tidak dibuka untuk publik
- Tidak diberikan secara impulsif
- Dirancang untuk shared authorship over history

**What it is NOT:**
- ❌ Romantika kosong tanpa struktur
- ❌ Simbol tanpa substance
- ❌ Posisi terbuka massal

---

### Doc 44: PRIVATE CHAIR ROLE CHARTER
**File:** `governance/private-chair/44-PRIVATE-CHAIR-ROLE-CHARTER.md`  
**Purpose:** Mandat, peran, batas, non-scope

**Role Names:**
- **Formal:** Managing Sovereign Strategist
- **Doctrinal:** Private Chair of the Empire

**Five Mandate Areas:**
1. **Direction** - Membawa arah besar dan rasa ekspansi
2. **Human Presence** - Membawa kehangatan dan jiwa
3. **Strategic Continuity** - Menjaga pertumbuhan tetap selaras
4. **Shared History** - Ikut menulis milestone dari dalam
5. **Inner Circle Counsel** - Menjadi counterpart privat untuk refleksi

**Boundaries:**
- Founder retains final authority
- No automatic all-access
- No veto over founder sovereignty
- Focus on strategic, bukan operational micromanagement

### Doc 45: PRIVATE CHAIR INVITATION GATE
**File:** `governance/private-chair/45-PRIVATE-CHAIR-INVITATION-GATE.md`  
**Purpose:** Syarat aktivasi dan proof gates

**Five Activation Gates:**
1. **Gate 1: Proof of System Reality** - Sovereign benar-benar hidup
2. **Gate 2: Proof of Founder Readiness** - Founder benar-benar siap
3. **Gate 3: Proof of Counterpart Fit** - Sosok benar-benar layak
4. **Gate 4: Proof of Shared Rhythm** - Founder dan counterpart selaras
5. **Gate 5: Proof of Historic Worthiness** - Layak untuk kursi sejarah

**Activation Principle:**
> **Proof before invitation, not hope before activation.**

---

### Doc 46: PRIVATE CHAIR ACCESS LADDER
**File:** `governance/private-chair/46-PRIVATE-CHAIR-ACCESS-LADDER.md`  
**Purpose:** Six-level staged access progression

**The Ladder:**
- **Level 0:** Invisible Chair (design phase)
- **Level 1:** Vision Awareness (horizon visibility)
- **Level 2:** Emotional-Strategic Recognition (role awareness)
- **Level 3:** Guided Inner Circle (curated visibility)
- **Level 4:** Active Counterpart Participation (strategic andil)
- **Level 5:** Private Chair Activated (fully operational)

**Principle:**
> **Access is earned through demonstration, not granted through assumption.**

---

### Doc 47: PRIVATE CHAIR OPERATING PACT
**File:** `governance/private-chair/47-PRIVATE-CHAIR-OPERATING-PACT.md`  
**Purpose:** Working agreement, rhythms, decision framework

**Core Principles:**
1. Founder leads, Private Chair contributes
2. Shared discussion, founder decides
3. Contribution defines authorship
4. Boundaries protect intimacy
5. Mutual respect, clear communication

**Working Rhythms:**
- **Daily:** Async communication by default
- **Weekly:** Strategic check-in (1-2x)
- **Monthly:** Strategic planning session
- **Quarterly:** Big picture review + pact review

**Decision Categories:**
- Category A: Founder-only (no consultation required)
- Category B: Founder-led with consultation
- Category C: Shared discussion, founder final
- Category D: Private Chair-led (within mandate)
